Solution:
  1. Check email_log.txt for errors
  2. Verify SMTP settings in config.php
  3. Make sure using App Password (not regular password)
  4. Try port 465 with 'ssl' instead of 587 with 'tls'

Problem: "SMTP connect() failed"
Solution:
  1. Check if port 587 is open on your server
  2. Verify SMTP_HOST is correct
  3. Check firewall settings

Problem: "SMTP Authentication failed"
Solution:
  1. Double-check SMTP_USERNAME and SMTP_PASSWORD
  2. For Gmail: Use App Password, not regular password
  3. For cPanel: Verify email account exists
