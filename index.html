ABOUT

Set sail on the Arabian Gulf's most exclusive yachting rally. Launching right after the energy of Race Weekend, Abu Dhabi Rendezvous departs from the iconic Yas Marina for an immersive voyage to the pristine shores of Sir Bani Yas Island. More than just a rally, it's a lifestyle experience, blending adventure, luxury, and cultural exploration.

Guests can sail at their own pace or join the friendly rally competition, creating the perfect balance between leisure and excitement. Along the way, enjoy bespoke hospitality, wildlife adventures, and an unforgettable Arabian BBQ under the stars.

Be part of a growing regional movement that champions coastal tourism and builds a community of passionate yacht lovers and adventurers.
